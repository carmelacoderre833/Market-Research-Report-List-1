<p><strong>The Automotive Die Casting Lubricants Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Die Lubricants</li><li>Plunger Lubricants</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive die casting lubricants market consists of three main types: die lubricants, plunger lubricants, and others. Die lubricants are applied to the mold surfaces to reduce friction and improve the release of cast parts, ensuring smoother operations. Plunger lubricants are used on the plunger mechanisms that inject molten metal into the molds, enhancing performance and reducing wear. Other lubricants may include release agents and additives that optimize the die casting process, enhance efficiency, and improve product quality.</p></p>
