<p><ul><li>Conventional Ultrasonic Flaw instruments</li><li>Phased Array ultrasonic testing instruments</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Ultrasonic Fault Detector market comprises two primary types: Conventional Ultrasonic Flaw Instruments and Phased Array Ultrasonic Testing Instruments. Conventional instruments utilize a single probe to detect and evaluate flaws in materials by measuring reflected sound waves. In contrast, Phased Array instruments employ multiple transducers to create detailed images and assess flaws with improved accuracy. The phased array technology offers enhanced versatility and the ability to inspect complex geometries, making it increasingly popular in various industrial applications for ensuring material integrity.</p></p>
